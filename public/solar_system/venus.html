Venus

Venus, the 2nd planet from the sun, has Sulfuric Acid Rain Surface Temperature:

  • Day: 864°F
  • Night: 864°F (Thick atmosphere does not allow cooling)

Made Of:

  • Surface: Iron and Rocks
  • Atmosphere: 96% Carbon Dioxide | 4% Nitrogen

Cycles:

  • Venus Day: 116 Days, 18 Hours (Earth Time)
  • Venus Year: 225 Days (Earth Time)
